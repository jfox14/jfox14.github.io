Hire Me(?)

Life is short, your time is valuable, and hiring a specialist is conufsing. Here's a quick way to determine whether or not we'd work well together.

Reach out to me if you:
  • Are interestd in a fixed delivery arrangement for UI/UX assets.
  • Have mid to high fidelity UI/UX mockups, but need help turning them into code.
  • Are in need of a custom ReactJS component library
  • Are interested in having me speak or train on my expertise
Don't reach out to me if:
  • Need to hire a fulltime UI developer
  • Need help in Javascript / React, but mostly of the business logic variety
  • Don't have solidified mockups of what you want your application's UX/UI to look like
